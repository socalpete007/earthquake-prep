1. Immediate Action: Drop, Cover, and Hold On

🏠 Indoors

DROP to your hands and knees. COVER your head and neck under a sturdy table or desk. HOLD ON to your shelter until the shaking stops.

🌳 Outdoors

Clear away from buildings, trees, streetlights, and power lines. Drop, cover, and cushion your head and neck with your arms.

🚗 Driving

Pull over safely away from overpasses, bridges, and wires. Engage the parking brake and stay inside until shaking completely ceases.

Safe Zones (Seek These Out) Danger Zones (Avoid!)
✔ Under sturdy desks or dining tables ✖ Near large windows, mirrors, or glass panels
✔ Against interior load-bearing walls ✖ Under heavy, unanchored bookshelves or wardrobes
✔ Hallways away from hanging light fixtures ✖ Directly inside the kitchen (falling dishes, gas connections)

4. Home Mitigation & Utility Controls

Gas Shut-Off Valve

Location

East side of house below the bathroom window.

Instructions: Use the dedicated crescent wrench attached straight to the piping. Turn the valve a quarter-turn in either direction until the crossbar profile stands entirely crosswise to the pipe.

Water Main Valve

Location

Front of house next to the garage and immediately below the water spicket.

Instructions: Turn the handwheel valve securely clockwise until rotation stops completely to isolate the infrastructure lines.

Main Electrical Panel

Location

Back of house against the wall and near the BBQ grill.

Instructions: Open outer sub-panel safety gate. Flip the distinct main circuit breaker toggle switch located firmly at the top layout down to the "OFF" position.
